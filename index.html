Frequently Asked Questions

Who needs this tool?

Marketers mostly, so they can look which competitors are mentioned by AI, in which order, what are the resources and websites mentioned by AI, etc.

How much does it cost?

The tool is free and open-source. You only need to pay for using AI models via selected provider (like OpenAI, Claude or OpenRouter).

How secure is it?

It runs on your computer. AICW sends questions to selected AI models via selected AI model provider - check Terms of Use and Privacy Policy of these providers for more information.

I want to add a feature or report an issue?

AICW tool is open-source, check the source code at GitHub and add a feature or report an issue.

Sample Calculation of AI Model Costs

Some providers may offer free access to certain AI models (for example, foundation models such as Llama, Qwen). Even when costs apply, the total can be very low â€“ often just a few cents for small research projects.

Example: For a project using 5 questions to query 3 major AI models (ChatGPT with search, Claude Sonnet with search, and Perplexity AI Sonar with search), plus OpenAI 4o-mini for enrichment/analysis, the total cost via OpenRouter as the AI provider was about $0.30 (30 cents).

  • AI models used: ChatGPT (with search), Claude Sonnet (with search), Perplexity AI Sonar (with search), OpenAI 4o-mini
  • Number of questions: 5
  • Total runs (questions x models): 5 × 3 = 15 main queries
  • + enrichment and analysis models for deeper insights
  • Provider in example: OpenRouter
  • Estimated total cost: $0.30
Note: You do not pay anything for using the AI Chat Watch tool itself on your computer. You only pay for queries sent to AI providers, if they are not free.
